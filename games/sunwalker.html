What is Sunwalker?

You play as Anne - a lone survivor. By guiding her in her adventure, you find clues about what happened to her and the world around her.

You guide her by staying in the shadows, where she is safe... ...you manipulate the environment around her to protect her from the sun... ...and find new friends along the way.

Sunwalker is a VR game comfortable for everyone to play, not causing any motion sickness, yet allowing the player to enjoy the immersive VR world.
